Native HRIS integrations supply org hierarchy and employee records — the
baseline used by [compliance automation](../capabilities/compliance-automation.md)
and by the [Nexus Radar early-warning engine](../nexus-radar/early-warning-engine.md)
(home/tax state and employment dates).
