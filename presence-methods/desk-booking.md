If an organization already uses "hoteling" or space-booking software such as
Skedda, Robin, or Archie, Atteniv can use this data to establish presence. See
[workspace booking integrations](../integrations/workspace-booking.md).

# How It Works

When an employee arrives at the office, they interact with their booking app to
"claim" or check into their reserved desk or room. Atteniv ingests this check-in
status as proof of presence.

# The Upside

Piggybacks on tools employees are already required to use for daily logistics.

# The Downside

Relies on human behavioral compliance: employees may arrive but simply forget to
hit the "check-in" button, creating false absences.
