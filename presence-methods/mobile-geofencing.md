Some enterprise HCM platforms (like Workday) use geofencing through a corporate
mobile app; Atteniv can support the same pattern.

# How It Works

If the company uses an Atteniv mobile app, the app can use location services to
detect when an employee crosses the "geofence" of the office building,
automatically checking them in.

# The Upside

Completely frictionless for the employee, as long as they have their phone in
their pocket.

# The Downside

Can trigger severe privacy concerns among employees who do not want their
employer tracking their smartphone's location services — a notable contrast with
the privacy-first [network detection](network-detection.md) method.
