Perhaps the simplest, lowest-barrier method: a tablet or scanning kiosk at the
office reception or entrance. QR-code generation for office check-in is built
into the [Office Management surface](../capabilities/office-optimization.md).

# How It Works

A digital barcode or QR code is automatically regenerated daily on the kiosk.
Employees use their mobile device to scan the code upon entering, which instantly
marks them as "present" in Atteniv.

# The Upside

Extremely easy to deploy, highly visible, and requires zero integration with
complex internal IT systems.

# The Downside

Adds a manual, administrative step to the employee's morning routine.
