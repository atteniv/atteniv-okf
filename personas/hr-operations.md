HR Operations and People Ops leaders enforce hybrid policies and support employee
moves, remote hires, and hybrid policy design.

# What They Need

* Enforce hybrid policies without surveillance and automate
  [progressive discipline](../concepts/progressive-discipline.md).
* Ask plain-English questions and get answers grounded in the actual footprint.
  *(US-9)*
* A pre-hire nexus check before extending an offer in a new state. *(US-10)*

# How Atteniv Serves Them

[Compliance automation](../capabilities/compliance-automation.md) with
accommodation-aware enforcement, [hybrid scheduling](../capabilities/hybrid-scheduling.md),
footprint-aware grounded chat (roadmap Tier 2), and the pre-hire what-if check
(roadmap Tier 4.1).
