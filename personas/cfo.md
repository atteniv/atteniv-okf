The CFO cares about total exposure and the second-largest line item on the books:
real estate.

# What They Need

* A single dashboard of total multi-state exposure for board reporting and M&A
  readiness. *(US-7)*
* An on-demand cited compliance summary that can be handed to diligence in under
  48 hours. *(US-8)*
* To right-size the real-estate footprint with evidence, not guesswork.

# How Atteniv Serves Them

Dashboard top-line metrics (FR-19), the board-ready compliance digest (roadmap
Tier 3.3 / FR-44), and [office optimization](../capabilities/office-optimization.md)
cost analysis via [NetSuite](../integrations/erp-cost.md).
