Facilities and Real Estate leaders decide how much space the organization needs
and must justify lease decisions.

# What They Need

* Right-size space using real utilization data, not assumed capacity.
* Justify lease renewals, consolidations, and terminations with evidence.
* Avoid both paying for empty desks and getting caught short on peak days.

# How Atteniv Serves Them

[Office optimization](../capabilities/office-optimization.md): real-time and
historical utilization, cost analysis, over-capacity alerts, and AI-assisted
stack planning that identifies underutilized leases.
