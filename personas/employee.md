The employee is the subject of presence detection and the beneficiary of
self-service tooling.

# What They Need

* View their personal schedule, submit remote-work waiver requests, and see their
  personal compliance score.
* A plain-English explanation of their own multi-state situation, so they stop
  filing payroll tickets. *(US-12)*

# How Atteniv Serves Them

The self-service portal and Employee Dashboard in
[hybrid scheduling](../capabilities/hybrid-scheduling.md), and the employee
self-service tax explainer (roadmap Tier 4.2 / FR-51), with disclaimers.
