General Counsel cares about legal defensibility — of both attendance enforcement
and tax positions.

# What They Need

* Every alert and answer to carry an immutable, cited audit trail, to defend
  against aggressive audits. *(US-11)*
* Enforcement that is accommodation-aware, so discipline never fires against a
  protected employee.

# How Atteniv Serves Them

Audit-snapshot alerts (FR-16), 7-year immutable retention of alerts and answers
(NFR-5), the accommodation-aware
[compliance automation](../capabilities/compliance-automation.md) engine, and the
Audit Defensible Kit that packages the full compliance record for outside
counsel.
