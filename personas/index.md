# Personas

The roles Atteniv serves, and what each one gets from the platform.

* [VP of Tax](vp-of-tax.md) - Prioritizes multi-state compliance actions from cited exposure.
* [Payroll Director](payroll-director.md) - Adjusts withholding before the payroll cycle; catches over-withholding.
* [CFO](cfo.md) - Board- and M&A-ready exposure reporting; right-sizes real estate.
* [HR Operations](hr-operations.md) - Enforces hybrid policy without surveillance; asks grounded questions.
* [Facilities / Real Estate](facilities-real-estate.md) - Right-sizes space with real utilization data.
* [Compliance Director](compliance-director.md) - Early visibility into multi-state regulatory risk.
* [General Counsel](general-counsel.md) - Immutable, cited audit trail for defensibility.
* [Employee](employee.md) - Self-service schedule, waivers, and a plain-English tax explainer.
