The VP of Tax owns the organization's multi-state [tax nexus](../concepts/tax-nexus.md)
exposure and is the primary user of [Nexus Radar](../capabilities/nexus-radar.md).

# What They Need

* See at a glance which states have active nexus and which are approaching
  thresholds, to prioritize compliance actions. *(US-1)*
* Have each alert cite the statute it is based on, so it can be trusted and
  defended. *(US-2)*
* Be told when a law changes in a state where the company has people, so they are
  never blindsided. *(US-3)*

# How Atteniv Serves Them

The [early-warning engine](../nexus-radar/early-warning-engine.md) surfaces
computed exposure with cited explanations; the Legislative Change Monitor
(roadmap Tier 3.1) delivers footprint-scoped impact briefs.
