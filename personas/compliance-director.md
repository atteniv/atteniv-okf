The Compliance Director is responsible for multi-state regulatory risk across the
workforce.

# What They Need

* Early visibility into multi-state [nexus](../concepts/tax-nexus.md) exposure
  before it becomes audit exposure.
* A defensible, current picture of obligations across every state where people
  work.

# How Atteniv Serves Them

[Nexus Radar](../capabilities/nexus-radar.md)'s early-warning alerts, the
registration-gap detector (FR-21), and citation + freshness on every displayed
fact (roadmap Tier 0.2).
