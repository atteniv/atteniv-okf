**Strategic Pillar III.** Creates and manages hybrid work schedules at scale,
ensuring in-office days are enforced against a system — not just a calendar
invitation. The schedules defined here are what
[compliance automation](compliance-automation.md) enforces against.

# What It Does

Lets HR and managers design, assign, and monitor hybrid schedules across the
whole organization, then feeds those schedules into presence-based enforcement.

# How It Works

* **Visual day-by-day schedule interface** for HR and managers.
* **Bulk employee assignment** by team, department, or policy group (e.g.,
  Sales: Mon/Tue; Engineering: Wed/Thu).
* **"What-if" capacity planning scenarios** to model different schedule
  configurations before publishing.
* **Automatic detection of unassigned employees** to prevent onboarding gaps.
* **Employee self-service portal** — view personal schedule, submit remote-work
  waiver requests, and see personal compliance score.

# Platform Surfaces

* **Team Schedules** — card-based schedule management with compliance-rate trend
  indicators, day-rule assignments, and inline editing.
* **Attendance Policy Builder** — design attendance policies with a live
  capacity-impact preview, so mandates can be right-sized before they are
  published.
* **Employee Dashboard** — 90-day attendance heatmap, compliance donut, pending
  requests, and infraction counts per individual.
