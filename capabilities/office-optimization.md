**Strategic Pillar IV.** Provides real-time and historical analytics on actual
office utilization from the [presence signal](presence-detection.md), enabling
data-driven decisions on real estate, staffing, and policy calibration. Real
estate is typically a company's second-largest expense.

# What It Does

Turns presence data into real-estate and capacity decisions: stop paying for
empty desks, and stop getting caught short on peak days. Space decisions become
evidence-based, not guesswork.

# How It Works

* **Real-time dashboards** — utilization by location, team, and date (e.g.,
  "Denver office at 33% capacity today").
* **Historical trends** — seasonal variation, day-of-week patterns, rolling
  averages.
* **NetSuite integration** — cost analysis for "true cost per employee per
  desk." See [ERP / cost integrations](../integrations/erp-cost.md).
* **Executive reporting** — compliance leaderboards, at-risk employee
  visibility, and cross-office comparisons.

# Platform Surfaces

* **Office Cost Analysis** — overlays monthly rent and operating expenses against
  actual per-employee attendance, surfacing the highest-cost office, the most
  efficient office by capacity, and the true cost per actual attendee.
* **Office Utilization Detail** — past-week utilization vs. total capacity,
  peak/lowest attendance days, over-capacity alerts, and team-schedule loadings
  per site.
* **Office Management** — bird's-eye view of every facility with current capacity
  utilization, active schedules, and QR-code badge check-in generation.
* **Attendance Analytics** — 30/90/180-day distribution of in-office, remote, and
  absent days, with org-tree drill-down by team and individual.

# AI-Assisted Optimization

With [Atteniv Intelligence](atteniv-intelligence.md), office optimization
extends to predictive capacity forecasting, smart schedule recommendations (to
prevent overcrowded Tuesdays and empty Fridays), and intelligent stack planning
to identify underutilized leases that can be safely terminated.
