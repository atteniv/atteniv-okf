Atteniv Intelligence is the **AI capability layer** built on top of the Atteniv
data platform. It is included in **every tier** — not a premium add-on. The
depth of insight scales with each tier.

# Tiers

## Essentials — Standard Intelligence

Basic compliance-summary queries and attendance reporting via natural language.

* "What's our current compliance rate?"
* "How many violations are open this week?"
* Attendance summaries by team or manager.

## Professional — Natural Language Analytics

Full natural-language query access across all platform data, so HR leaders get
answers in real time without involving IT.

* "What is the average attendance rate across all offices this quarter?"
* "Which teams have the most violations in the last 30 days?"
* "How many employees are currently on accommodation plans?"
* Historical trend analysis and utilization reporting.

## Enterprise — Master Atteniv Intelligence

A fundamentally deeper product — not just answering questions, but anticipating
problems and generating strategic-level insight.

* **Proactive risk detection** — flags at-risk employees *before* a violation
  triggers escalation; surfaces pattern anomalies such as a compliance drop
  correlated with a specific manager or department.
* **Executive-grade reporting** — auto-generates compliance narrative reports for
  board or executive review; cross-entity and multi-subsidiary comparisons.
* **Industry benchmarking** — anonymized peer-data benchmarking ("How does our
  compliance rate compare to similar companies?").
* **Custom query building** — save and schedule recurring queries; API access to
  the Intelligence layer for BI tools (Tableau, Power BI, etc.).

## Strategic — Master Atteniv Intelligence, Custom

All Enterprise capabilities, plus org-specific model tuning, custom intelligence
pipelines, and fully bespoke reporting architecture.

# Strategic AI Directions

Atteniv Intelligence anchors a broader roadmap that shifts attendance from
reactive tracking to predictive management:

* **Proactive risk & anomaly detection** — detect "compliance cascades"
  (non-compliance starting with a specific VP and trickling down) and "policy
  drift" (a slow multi-month decline no single weekly report catches).
* **Multi-state tax & nexus defense** — an "Anomaly Engine" runs daily network
  location logs against 50-state threshold logic to warn finance teams before an
  employee triggers [tax nexus](../concepts/tax-nexus.md) or international
  Permanent Establishment risk. This is the intelligence behind
  [Nexus Radar](nexus-radar.md).
* **Real-estate & capacity optimization** — predictive forecasting and smart
  schedule recommendations; see [office optimization](office-optimization.md).
* **Employee-experience "magnet" approach** — smart collaboration nudges and
  automated exception routing to reduce the "hybrid coordination tax."
