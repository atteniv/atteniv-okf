**Coffee badging** is swiping in (or being seen) at the office and leaving within
minutes — long enough to register as "present" but not long enough to actually
work on site. It is common and largely invisible to badge- and access-based
tools.

# Why It Matters to Atteniv

Coffee badging is the core reason badge data is *gameable* and why the
[Atteniv platform](../products/atteniv-platform.md) leads with
[network presence detection](../presence-methods/network-detection.md) rather
than [badge swipes](../presence-methods/badge-swipe.md). Methods that track stay
*duration* — such as [Wi-Fi access point detection](../presence-methods/wifi-access-point.md) —
are specifically valuable for combating it. See the
[mandate-compliance gap](mandate-compliance-gap.md).
