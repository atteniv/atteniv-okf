**Progressive discipline** is the staged escalation of consequences for repeated
attendance violations — moving from a verbal warning through a written warning
and a final warning to a termination review, with documentation at every step.

# Default Escalation Ladder

| Violations | Stage |
|-----------|-------|
| 4 | Verbal warning |
| 6 | Written warning |
| 8 | Final warning |
| 9 | Termination review |

Thresholds are fully configurable per company policy.

# Why It Matters to Atteniv

[Compliance automation](../capabilities/compliance-automation.md) automates this
lifecycle — auto-generating templated warning letters, tracking acknowledgment,
and (critically) excluding ADA and intermittent-FMLA accommodations from the
violation counter *before* any action fires. The full record is built for legal
defensibility; see the [general counsel persona](../personas/general-counsel.md).
