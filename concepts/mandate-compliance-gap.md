The **mandate-compliance gap** is the distance between "policy exists" and
"policy is enforced." Corporate RTO requirements rose roughly **12% since early
2024**, but actual attendance has barely moved — what analysts call the *Great
Office Standoff*.

# Why the Gap Persists

* Managers spend ~40% of their day on administrative tasks, much of it
  attendance tracking, follow-up, and documentation.
* Badge data is gameable via [coffee badging](coffee-badging.md).
* HR teams manage violations in spreadsheets with no consistent escalation path.
* ADA accommodations and FMLA plans live in separate systems, creating legal
  exposure when enforcement fires against protected employees.
* Real estate decisions are made on assumed capacity, not actual utilization.
