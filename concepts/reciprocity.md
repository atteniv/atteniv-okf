**Reciprocity agreements** are arrangements between states that simplify
withholding for employees who live in one state and work in another — typically
allowing the employee to be taxed and withheld only by their **home** state,
eliminating nonresident withholding in the work state.

# Why It Matters to Atteniv

Reciprocity is both a *suppressor* and an *opportunity* in
[Nexus Radar](../capabilities/nexus-radar.md):

* The [early-warning engine](../nexus-radar/early-warning-engine.md) suppresses
  nonresident-withholding obligations for reciprocal home→work pairs before
  evaluating a rule.
* The Reciprocity & Safe Harbor Navigator (roadmap Tier 3.2) surfaces
  **over-withholding** — money the org is needlessly withholding on a reciprocal
  pair — and flags expired agreements.
