Atteniv's unique position: the **only platform that combines non-invasive
[presence detection](../capabilities/presence-detection.md) + compliance
automation + accommodation management** in a single system — and, with
[Nexus Radar](../capabilities/nexus-radar.md), connects all of it to
state-by-state tax rules.

# Category Comparison

| Category | Example tools | What they do | What they miss |
|----------|---------------|--------------|----------------|
| Space / Facilities | Envoy, Robin, Skedda, Kadence, OfficeSpace | Desk booking, room reservations, badge check-ins | No compliance automation, no progressive discipline, no ADA/FMLA management; badge data gameable |
| HRIS | Workday, UKG, BambooHR | Time tracking, HR records, scheduling | No actual presence detection, no automated escalation, no accommodation-aware enforcement |
| Badge / Access Control | HID, Brivo, Proxy | Entry/exit tracking | Shows entry not presence; no compliance workflow; gameable |
| Manual / Spreadsheet | Current state for most companies | Free, familiar | Inconsistent, legally fragile, time-intensive, no audit trail, blind to accommodations |
| Payroll-vendor nexus guides | — | Generic reference content | Not connected to the actual workforce footprint |

# The Differentiators

* The enforcement engine knows who the exceptions are *before* it acts.
* The audit trail is built for legal defensibility.
* At scale, it surfaces workforce patterns and [policy drift](mandate-compliance-gap.md)
  invisible to any other tool.
