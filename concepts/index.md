# Concepts

Domain glossary for the terms used throughout this bundle.

* [Tax Nexus](tax-nexus.md) - The connection that gives a state authority to impose tax obligations on a business.
* [Convenience-of-the-Employer Rule](convenience-of-the-employer-rule.md) - Rule that can tax remote days as if worked in the employer's state.
* [Reciprocity](reciprocity.md) - Agreements between states that simplify withholding for cross-border commuters.
* [P.L. 86-272](pl-86-272.md) - Federal protection against state income tax that remote non-sales work can void.
* [Coffee Badging](coffee-badging.md) - Swiping in and leaving to appear present; defeats badge-based tracking.
* [Mandate-Compliance Gap](mandate-compliance-gap.md) - The gap between RTO policy on paper and enforced attendance.
* [Progressive Discipline](progressive-discipline.md) - The staged attendance disciplinary lifecycle Atteniv automates.
* [Competitive Landscape](competitive-landscape.md) - How Atteniv compares to facilities, HRIS, badge, and monitoring tools.
* [Model Context Protocol (MCP)](model-context-protocol.md) - Open protocol for connecting agents to external tools and data.
