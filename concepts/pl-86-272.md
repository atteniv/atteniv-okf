**P.L. 86-272** is a federal law that protects a business from a state's net
income tax when its only activity in that state is soliciting orders for tangible
personal property (with orders approved and fulfilled from outside the state).

# Why It Matters to Atteniv

A single remote employee performing **non-sales** work in an unregistered state
can **void** P.L. 86-272 protection and trigger full corporate income
[tax nexus](tax-nexus.md) — often **retroactively**. This is one of the hidden
multi-state tax risks the [Atteniv platform](../products/atteniv-platform.md)
exists to surface: presence data reveals exactly this kind of activity before a
state auditor does.
