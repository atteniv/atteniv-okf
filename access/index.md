# Access Channels

The Nexus knowledge corpus — shared by [Nexus Vision](../products/nexus-vision.md)
and [Nexus Radar](../capabilities/nexus-radar.md) — is reachable three ways.

* [Nexus Vision Web App](nexus-vision-web.md) - Free public research copilot for HR, payroll, and finance.
* [Public REST API](rest-api.md) - Versioned browse plus authenticated grounded Q&A.
* [MCP Server](mcp-server.md) - Drop the corpus into Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-capable agent.
