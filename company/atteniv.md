Atteniv builds hybrid-workforce management software that helps organizations
understand and manage where and how their people work. The platform combines
employee **presence tracking** with **regulatory intelligence** to help HR and
payroll teams stay ahead of compliance risk in a distributed-work world.

Atteniv is headquartered in Denver, CO. Heidi Brown serves as Chief Marketing
Officer and Interim CEO.

# What Atteniv Is

Atteniv is a **policy enforcement and risk management platform for hybrid
organizations**. It answers one question: *is your attendance policy being
followed — consistently, fairly, and in a way that holds up legally?*

Atteniv is **not** a productivity tool. It does not measure work output or
performance, and it does not add invasive monitoring. It operates entirely
within the security and HR infrastructure an organization already has — no new
hardware, no surveillance software, no behavior tracking.

The company ships two products:

* The [Atteniv platform](../products/atteniv-platform.md) — the paid,
  hybrid-workforce management product.
* [Nexus Vision](../products/nexus-vision.md) — a free, public remote-work tax
  research service.

# The Problem It Solves

## The Mandate-Compliance Gap

Corporate return-to-office requirements have increased roughly **12% since early
2024**, but actual attendance has not moved — a phenomenon analysts call the
*Great Office Standoff*. The gap between "policy exists" and "policy is enforced"
is unaddressed by standard HR and facilities tooling. See the
[mandate-compliance gap](../concepts/mandate-compliance-gap.md) concept for why
the gap persists.

Employers face three simultaneous failures:

1. **Compliance gap** — Policies exist on paper but are not enforced.
   [Coffee badging](../concepts/coffee-badging.md) is common and invisible to
   most tools.
2. **Real estate misalignment** — Companies pay for unused space or scramble for
   desks on peak days. Decisions are expensive guesswork without utilization
   data.
3. **Hidden multi-state tax risk** — A single remote employee performing
   non-sales work in an unregistered state can void
   [P.L. 86-272](../concepts/pl-86-272.md) protections and trigger full
   corporate income [tax nexus](../concepts/tax-nexus.md), often retroactively.

# Guiding Principle

> Think of Atteniv as a smart thermostat for the modern office: it senses real
> occupancy and adjusts policy, space, and compliance posture automatically,
> without manual check-ins or self-reported badges.

# Guardrail

Atteniv does not provide tax advice and does not determine tax liability or
replace tax counsel. Atteniv provides presence data, state-specific
intelligence, and early risk visibility for review by the customer and their tax
professionals.
